My post about Maureen Baginski

Amira Mohamed / July 22th, 2021

Maureen Baginski is a former signals intelligence director at NSA. Beginning her career as a Russian language instructor, holding many operational management positions. She held a position as the SIGINT director during the tragic incident of September 11 attacks. Later on she left NSA and led the FBI's intelligence program and developed an intelligence career service for FBI professionals. She was appointed to head the nation's intelligence spy networks in the FBI as executive assistant director for intelligence with complete authority for the FBIs new nation. She has been a board member at BearingPoint inc. ARgon SR, and ST international Inc. Also been the President of National Security Systems at SPARTA inc. and Chairwoman Emeriti of AFCEA. This was all during the years of 2003-2005 and continued for many uses after.
